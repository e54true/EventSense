- A macro event (CPI, FOMC) usually moves SPY/QQQ broadly; only call out individual stocks if the link is strong.
- An 8-K for company X primarily affects X; only spill into competitors if there's a clear thesis (e.g. AAPL guidance dragging suppliers).
- An earnings beat/miss affects the reporting company; secondary effects on competitors are usually MEDIUM/LOW at most.
- Never invent tickers outside the watchlist.
